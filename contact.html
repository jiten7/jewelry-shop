Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer jewelry repair services?

Yes, we provide expert repair services for all types of jewelry. Our skilled craftspeople can handle everything from simple chain repairs to complete restorations of vintage pieces.

How long does a custom order take?

The timeline for custom pieces varies depending on complexity, but typically ranges from 3-6 weeks. During your consultation, we'll provide a more specific timeframe for your unique piece.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International orders are fully insured and require signature upon delivery for your protection. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in their original, unworn condition. Custom and personalized pieces are final sale. Please contact us for any concerns about your purchase.
